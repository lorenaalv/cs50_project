About Buying on the Road

Introduction

What We Do: Buying on the Road is an innovative website application designed for the nostalgic traveler. Our platform empowers you to track your purchases and pin point them on a map. Post-roadtrip, you'll have all your purchases mapped, showing the long road you've taken!

Our Mission: Our mission is to ensure you have all your (material) memories in one place, mapping the road you've traveled.

Our Story

Background: Buying on the Road was born from the idea that travelers like to reminisce on their memories, whether through photos or the souvenirs they bought.

Evolution: Since the formation of our project, Buying on the Road has grown from a simple idea into a fully-fledged application serving a diverse user base. We've stumbled across inveitable bumps in the ROAD ;) but ultimately were able to meet our goal and create a website application that allows you to view your purchases on the road! We are also continuously updating our platform with new features. Our application is a commitment to improving travel experiences.
