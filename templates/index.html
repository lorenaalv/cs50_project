About Buying on the Road

Introduction

What We Do: Buying on the Road is a website application that allows you to track your purchases on the road virtually anywhere. Have you ever traveled and wondered when your packages would arrive to your doorstep? Well, with Buying on the Road you will be able to pinpoint exactly where your purchases are!

Our Mission: Our mission is to make it more convenient to track your purchases while on the road. Instead of having to wait and guess the arrival time, you can just input your purchase information through a form and see exactly where that purchase is.
